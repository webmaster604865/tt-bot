## Usage Instructions

To utilize the **Facebook Friend Search** feature within MadHub, follow these steps:

1. **Access the Feature**: Launch MadHub and navigate to the Facebook Friend Search section.
2. **Set Filtering Criteria**:
   - **Keywords**: Specify keywords related to your target audience.
   - **Filters**: Apply additional filters such as location, mutual friends count, and gender to refine your search results.
3. **Operation Mode**: Choose whether to perform actions in **input mode** (manually enter details) or **file mode** (upload a file with user data).
4. **Initiate Search**: Click on the search button to begin the process. MadHub will automatically gather users that fit your specified criteria.
